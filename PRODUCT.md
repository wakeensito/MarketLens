# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Early-stage founders and solo builders validating a business idea before committing time or money. They're in decision mode — often late at night, alone, running the numbers in their head. They need a straight answer: is this worth pursuing, and where does it fit in the market? They don't need encouragement; they need credible intelligence.

## Product Purpose

MarketScout is an AI-powered market intelligence tool. A user types a business idea and receives a competitive landscape, saturation score, and entry roadmap — fast. Success looks like a founder walking away with a clear picture of whether their idea has room to grow, who they're up against, and what the first moves should be. The product replaces hours of manual research with a single, structured analysis.

## Brand Personality

Sharp, credible, fast. Like a senior research analyst who never wastes your time. Outputs are confident; framing is neutral. The product doesn't cheer or warn — it informs.

References: Stripe Dashboard (data-rich, professional, structured, trustworthy) and Perplexity (research-first, clean answer surfaces, smart assistant energy without the chat bubble aesthetic).

## Anti-references

- Generic SaaS dashboard: pastel cards, excessive rounding, friendly iconography, "Get started" CTA energy.
- AI chatbot interface: full-screen chat UI, streaming text front-and-center, conversational framing that makes the product feel like a toy.
- Growth-hacker marketing tools: gradient CTAs, excitement copy, over-animation, "10x your revenue" tone.

## Design Principles

1. **Conclusions over process** — surface the insight, not the machinery. A founder shouldn't have to interpret the output; they should be able to act on it immediately.
2. **Precision earns trust** — every element on screen should carry meaning. No decorative patterns, no filler motion, no ambient glow for aesthetics.
3. **Confident but not loud** — the interface speaks like an expert advisor, not a salesperson. Dense where density is warranted; spacious where clarity matters more.
4. **Speed as signal** — the UI should feel as fast as the intelligence it delivers. Unnecessary transitions or loading states erode confidence in the product.
5. **Research-grade structure** — reports should read like professional deliverables. Numbered sections, clear hierarchy, scannable in 30 seconds and readable in 5 minutes.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum. Adequate contrast ratios across all text sizes. Full keyboard navigation for the search flow and report view. Respect `prefers-reduced-motion` for all animations.
